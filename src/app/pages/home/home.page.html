Reusable Mask

The Arena Tour Mask is the first & only American-made, N95/PM2.5 filter insert compatible, reusable face mask. Tested and iterated in-the-field with Phoenix's largest medical groups and law enforcement agencies. 100% Manufactured in the USA. Comfortable. Durable. Includes adjustable nose bridge. Top elastic tie-back / Bottom elastic neck strap. Machine wash and reuse 50+ times. Suitable for industrial sanitizing.

Composition: 2-part outer & inner shell, 100% polyester
Washing: Machine wash and reuse 50+ times. Suitable for industrial sanitizing
Strap Options: Available with tie-backs and/or elastic straps
Optional 3M N95 filter media insert or PM2.5 Carbon filter insert

The mask is designed to hold a compatible 3M proprietary filter media full-mask insert or a PM2.5 disposable carbon filter. Inserts can be replaced through the side seams of the mask and center into place between the vertical darts on the nose bridge and chin. The inner shell is the pocket for the disposable inserts.

PM 2.5 Carbon Filter Size: 12cm * 8 cm / 4.72in * 3.15 in
3M N95 Filter Filter Media Size: 24.8cm * 15.6cm  / 9.75in * 6.15in
Stretchable & Breathable

The mask is a 2-piece premium composition, with a reversible design. The 4-way stretch fabric has high performance fibers that support the body’s natural cooling process by wicking away moisture evenly throughout the construction of the fabric.

The Tour Mask is constructed using a 2-piece premium composition, with a reversible design. The 4-way stretch fabric has high performance fibers that support the body’s natural cooling process by wicking away moisture evenly throughout the construction of the fabric. The outside polyester shell maintains the shape and provides an additional layer of filtration for large particles.

Outer shell: 100% Polyester (250 gsm / 3mm thick)
Inner shell: 82% Poly / 18% Spandex (240 gsm / 0.35mm thick)
